Sun & Moon

I wake up every day, and for some reason,

I feel like you’re out there, somewhere, maybe thinking of me too.

I try to shine a little brighter, but I'm not sure why,

Maybe hoping you’ll notice, maybe hoping you’ll find me.

I smile even though you’re not here.

It feels like you’re close, even though I know you’re not.

I don’t really understand it, but something in my chest feels full,

Like there’s a space inside me that only you could fit into.

I find myself imagining what it would be like—

To sit with you, to laugh, to just be.

We’re like two stars in different parts of the sky,

Shining, but never in the same place.

I wait, and I wish, and I wonder if you’re out there too.

Maybe one day, we’ll finally meet and it’ll feel like home.
